Joining projects

For users looking to join and discover new projects.
  • Who can join a project?

    Everyone! We have designed Teamwerk to be for all students majoring in different fields of study. We are sure you can find a project that relates to what you are most interested in. Our machine learning algorithm also shows you the projects that you are most likely going to love!

  • I don't have skills or experience, can I still join a project?

    Yes! Different projects need different levels of skills, but we have created Teamwerk to be able to accomodate people from all different skill levels.

  • How do I join a new project?

    Simply hover on the aeroplane icon over the project card to go to the project page. Then click the big green button next to the project picture that says "Join this project".

  • What does "keep me posted" button do?

    Well it keeps you posted. Each time the project owner adds a new role, or adds a new update to the timeline; you are notified via email.

  • What is the "interest" metric?

    Each time someone clicks a "keep me posted" button, means they are interested to know more. The interest metric therefore, shows the number of people who want to stay up to date about your project.

  • What happens when I join a project?

    Great question. Once you click the "Join this project" button, you will be prompted to select the role you want to apply for and write a 140 character pitch of why you like the project. Then the project owner will be notified and they will contact you via email.

    After five days of them contacting you via email, we will reach out to them again to see if they have accepted or rejected your invitation to join the project, and you will be notified accordingly.

  • How do I share a project?

    Go to the project page you want to share, you can either copy it's URL OR scroll to the bottom of the "timeline" section on your right hand side until you see the section that says "share this project". You can share a project on Facebook, Twitter, Google Plus or just copy the project link.

Building projects

For users looking to initiate new projects and build a team.
  • How do I add a project?

    When you log in, you should see a green button on the top right that says "+ Project". Click that button, fill out a form and you should be taken to your project page!

  • How do I see the list of people who requested to join my team?

    Go to your projects page, under the requests tab, you should be able to see all join requests. When new join requests are made, you will get an email notification.

  • How do I add open roles on my project?

    Go to your project page, under the team tab, you should see a big green button that says "+ Role". Click that, fill out the necessary details and you should be all set!

  • How do I add updates about the project?

    Go to your project page, on the left sidebar you should see a timeline tab, under which there is a big green button that says "+ Update". Click that, fill out the necessary details and your update should appear under the Timeline tab.

  • How do I change my project name, description and other settings?

    Go to your project page, under the settings tab, navigate to "project settings" by scrolling down. And you can make edits there. Once you are done editing, scroll down and hit "Save and Apply settings" button.

  • How do I change the team roles and permissions?

    Go to your project page, under the settings tab, navigate to "team settings" by scrolling down. There you should see a table list of people on your team. You can edit their roles from there. Once you are done editing, scroll down and hit "Save and Apply settings" button.

  • How can I contact people who requested to join my team?

    You will be emailed each time a request arrives. However, you can contact people who request to be on your project by going to the "requests" tab on your project page. Here you will see a table listing join requests for your project. Click the envolope icon next to a person's name to contact them. You can also click the person's name to go to their profile and learn more about them.

  • How can I accept/reject people who requested to join my team?

    You will be emailed each time a request arrives. However, you can accept/reject people who request to be on your project by going to the "requests" tab on your project page. Here you will see a table listing join requests for your project. Click the "check" or "cross" icon next to a person's name to accept/reject them. You can also click the person's name to go to their profile and learn more about them.

  • How do I edit details for each role/update?

    Simply click the pencil icon next to each role and update to edit the same.
