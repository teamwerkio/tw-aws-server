TLDR. Be respectful. Harassment and abuse are never tolerated. If you are come across a project that makes you uncomfortable on Teamwerk, if the project itself is creating an unsafe or inappropriate environment, or if interacting with a project Owner or project Member makes you uncomfortable, please report it using the procedures included in this document.


Teamwerk stands for inclusivity. We believe that every single person has the right to share their ideas in a safe and welcoming environment.


Harassment includes but is not limited to offensive verbal or written comments related to gender, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, religion, sexual images on project pages, deliberate intimidation, stalking, following, harassing photography or recording, sustained disruption, and unwelcomed sexual attention. If what you’re doing is making someone feel uncomfortable, that counts as harassment and is enough reason to stop doing it.


In particular, users should not use sexualized images, activities, or other material both on their projects and during the event.


If a user engages in harassing behavior, Teamwerk may take any action Teamwerk deems appropriate, including warning the offender or expulsion from the website with no eligibility for of any type.


If you are being harassed, notice that someone else is being harassed, or have any other concerns, please contact Teamwerk using the reporting procedures defined below.


Teamwerk representatives will be happy to help users contact campus security or local law enforcement those experiencing harassment to feel safe for any incident that occurred on the Teamwerk website. We value your engagement.


We expect users to follow these rules on all online interactions in relation to the projects and on other platforms where connections were made by Teamwerk.


Reporting Procedures

If you feel uncomfortable or think there may be a potential violation of this code of conduct, please report it immediately using one of the following methods. All reporters have the right to remain anonymous.


By sending information to the general reporting line, your report will go to any or all of the Teamwerk representatives listed below.


  • General reporting line: report@teamwerk.io
  • Taafaki Moore (Teamwerk Representative): taafaki@teamwerk.io

Teamwerk reserves the right to revise, make exceptions to, or otherwise amend these policies in whole or in part. If you have any questions regarding these policies, please contact Teamwerk by e-mail at info@teamwerk.io
